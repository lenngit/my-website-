    Broad Recommendations

    What High-Performing Firms Do

    High-performing elder law firms respond within 60 seconds, allow self-scheduling without phone contact, and run automated follow-up sequences calibrated to their case type. They know their lead-to-consultation and consultation-to-retainer conversion rates every month. They treat intake as a system, not a task.

    Three Actions You Can Take Today

    01

    Measure your current numbers

    Pull last month's leads, consultations booked, and retained clients. If you cannot locate these numbers in under two minutes, that tells you something important about the state of your pipeline visibility.

    02

    Time your own response

    Submit a test inquiry through your website contact form. Note what happens, when it happens, and whether it would happen the same way on a Saturday evening. That is your lead's experience.

    03

    Map the gap from first contact to retainer

    Write every step a lead takes from initial inquiry to signed agreement. Count how many require someone in your office to manually act. That number is your structural exposure.
